Eye-Tracking and Visualization Tools

By group 39

What to find on this site.

Eye-tracking can be visualized in different ways. First of all, you need to store the data of the eye-tracking to make visualizations out of it. With eye-tracking visualizations you could for instance see where the visual interests of people lie on, for example, a movie poster.

On this page you can find the four different visualization tools you can use to visualize eye-tracking. The different kinds of visualizations and their descriptions can be found under the head "Visualization". On this site you can make of your own eye-tracking visualizations. To do this, you need to go to the head "Upload", and upload your data and choose the visualization you want.
