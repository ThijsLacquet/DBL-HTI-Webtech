How to upload the data?

With usage of the upload button you can make a visualization. You can choose between four different visualization for your eye-tracking data. In the upload button you need to give your eye-tracking data. If you inserted your data in the upload button, you need to make a choice which visualization you want your data in. If you go to the visualization page you can find the description of the different kinds of visualizations. If you made your choice in visualization you need to press the button "visualize" and you can find your visualization bellow.
