How to upload the data?

With usage of the upload button you can make a visualization. You can choose between four different visualizations for your eye-tracking data. You can find the description of the different kinds of visualizations on the information page. With the upload button you can sync your eye-tracking data. Once, you succesfully uploaded the data, you will automatically be redirected to the visualization page. Uploading may take some time, so please be patient.
