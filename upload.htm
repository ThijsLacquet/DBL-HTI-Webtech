How to upload the data?

With usage of the upload button you can make a visualization. You can choose between four different visualizations for your eye-tracking data. You can find the description of the different kinds of visualizations on the visualization page. With the upload button you can sync your eye-tracking data. You can find the programmed visualizations and the tools on the test page.
